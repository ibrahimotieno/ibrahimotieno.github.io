Shuklo

A large marketplace for Shoes and Clothes.

Shuklo is a free outfit-ad-listing platform where users looking for outfits, both shoes and clothes, reach sellers of such products faster. Sellers on the other hand get to share their products for free and once verified, are made available in the app for potential buyers.
